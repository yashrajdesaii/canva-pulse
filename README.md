### What is Canva Pulse?
An always-on intelligent agent that eliminates last-mile brand compliance friction in Canva Teams & Enterprise. It watches live collaboration, detects micro-drift in real time, offers Auto-Heal suggestions, and learns from Brand Admin feedback — empowering local franchise teams to move fast while protecting central governance.

### My AI-Native Workflow
I treated this as a real product problem and ran it through a full multi-agent AI workflow:

- **Framing** – ChatGPT helped create a structured Product Thesis framework from the PDF and Eulerity JD.
- **Ideation & Strategy** – Google AI Studio for deep brainstorming and creative pressure-testing.
- **Research & Grounding** – Grok for synthesizing real 2026 Canva case studies, G2 reviews, Reddit signals, and accurate data.
- **Synthesis** – Continuous back-and-forth between agents, discarding reactive ideas until the final vision was locked.
- **Execution** – Generated the full presentation with Kimi and built the interactive prototype in Lovable (leveraging my experience with Lovable, Base44, and Codex).

This wasn’t “using AI tools.” It was **orchestrating AI as my primary interface** for discovery, research, synthesis, design, and shipping.

Built with focus on thoughtful product thinking, meaningful AI work, and premium execution.
